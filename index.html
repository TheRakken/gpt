Business courses

Objectives of our business programs:

  • Prepare students with the necessary business communication skills.
  • To foster a student thinking and problem solving skills and thus promote entrepreneurial initiative.
  • Develop the students' interpersonal, analytical and technical skills for the business environment.
  • To equip a student with basic proffesional skills such as planning , organizing, coordinating , motivating, staffing and controlling a business enterprise's activities.

Front-Office courses

Gladpoint College offers courses that seek to produce qualified front-office personnel. Every organization will always have the front office and thus the need to give an exceptionally good impression of the organization especially for first time visitors.

Objectives of our front-office programs:

  • Impart the necessary knowledge to a student to introduce them to office operations
  • Produce graduates who can excellently serve in diverse companies
  • Equip students with computer skills required by a typical office

ict courses

Information and communication technology is a vital resource to all sectors in the current society. Gladpoint college trains on the theoretical, practical and technical aspects of ICT that are satisfactorily geared towards helping our students manage information systems. Because of the dynamic and complex nature of ICT, skilled personnel are continually needed.

Objectives of our ICT programs:

  • Empower learners with both theoretical and practical skills in an integrated manner and improve their problem solving skills.
  • Impart practical business skills to students that will help them start and run ICT-based businesses.

language courses

With globalization, people from different regions of the world often need to interact. Gladpoint offers training in different languages including French, German, English, Swahili, and others.

Objectives of our language programs:

  • Empower learners with essential business communication skills
  • Impart basic culture skills associated with the origin of the language
