Secretarial Courses

Computerized Secretarial

The college handles courses that seek to produce qualified personnel who will be in charge of the front office. Every organization will always have the front office and thus need for individuals to be fully trained to give an exceptionally good impression of the organization and especially the first time.

Objectves:

  • Impart the necessary knowledge to a student to open them to the required skills for the office.
  • Produce graduates who can excellently serve in any company regardless of their diversity.
  • Train students to climb up the ladder of success to become personnel assistant and permanent secretaries
  • Train students to exceptionally high typing speeds for efficient typing.
  • The student should demonstrate confidence in using presentation tools in a computer at the end of course.
These personnel may include; receptionists, secretaries or customer service assistants. It recommended that students are trained to fit in any field and thus widening their chances.
