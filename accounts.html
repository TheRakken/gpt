Business and Accounting Courses

Accounting Technician Certification

The ATC certificate provides a person with comprehensive and widely recognised qualification which enables the holder to perform functions in an accounting, auditing, business or public office.

Certified Public Accountant

The CPA examination is benchmarked on the international standards promulgated by International Accounting Education Standards Board (IAESB), International Accounting Standards Board (IASB), International Auditing and Assurance Standards Board (IAASB) and International Public Sector Accounting Standards Board (IPSASB). The CPA examination therefore meets the requirements of International Education Standards (IESs), International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRSs), International Accounting Standards (IASs), International Standards on Auditing (ISAs) and International Public Sector Accounting Standards (IPSASs).

Business Administration/Management

The department involves the provision of knowledge and skills for the effective management and prosperity of business and their undertakings. Managerial role is vital in analyzing change in technology, completion, size and complexity of business of business firms. Diploma and certificate courses offered in the department include; business administration, human resources, management, accounting & finance, sales and marketing among others.

Objectves:

  • Equip students with the knowledge & skills necessary to secure job in the business industry.
  • Prepare students to with necessary business communication skills.
  • Develops a students interpersonal, analytical and technical skills blended for the business environment.
  • To foster a student thinking and problem solving skills and thus promote entrepreneurial initiative.
  • To equip a student with proper ways of planning , organizing, coordinating , motivating, staffing and controlling of business enterprise activities.
The business sector continues to grow and expand and therefore qualified graduates in the field of banking , sales , purchasing, marketing, accounting, finance, and human resources will continually be required so as to service the needs of this crucial sector.
